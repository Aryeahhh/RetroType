About RetroType

Established in London in 2018, RetroType for Typewriters is dedicated to making the timeless art of typewriting accessible to everyone, whether you're a novice or a seasoned enthusiast.

Recognizing that typewriters can be an unfamiliar territory for some, we strive to demystify this classic form of communication. At RetroType, we are committed to guiding you through every step of your typewriting journey because we believe that everyone, regardless of experience, can find joy in the rhythmic clicks of typewriter keys.

All of our typewriters undergo meticulous restoration, testing, and come with a guaranteed working condition, thanks to our team of skilled professionals. With over 12,000 typewriters sold worldwide and being the global top seller on platforms like Depop, you can trust RetroType for Typewriters to provide you with a quality and reliable product.

Beyond being a typewriter retailer, we view our mission as a preservation effort. We aim to rescue as many second-hand typewriters as possible from ending up in landfills, giving them a new lease on life for a whole new generation. Our passion lies in preserving the charm and authenticity of typewriting, allowing individuals to capture moments in a way that only typewriters can.

RetroType for Typewriters isn't just about selling typewriters; it's about fostering a love for the art of typewriting and ensuring these iconic machines continue to tell stories for years to come. Whether you're a curious beginner or a seasoned collector, RetroType for Typewriters is here to be your guide into the world of timeless typing. Start your typewriting journey with us!
